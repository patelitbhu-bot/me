About Municipal Help Desk

A Municipal Help Desk is a service provided by the local government to help citizens report and solve city-related problems. It acts as a single point where people can ask for help and register complaints.

Types of Complaints

Garbage collection and cleanliness issues

Water leakage or irregular water supply

Broken roads and pothole problems

Street lights not working

How can People report their problems?

  • By visiting the Municipal Help Desk
  • By submitting a written or verbal complaint
  • Through online portals
  • By calling on the help line numbers

The Lucknow Nagar Nigam Website

The Lucknow Nagar Nigam website is an official online platform provided by the municipal corporation to help citizens access civic services easily.
Residents can use this portal to access various municipal services, including:

  • House Tax Services
  • Property IDs
  • Forms and Bylaws
  • Grievances
